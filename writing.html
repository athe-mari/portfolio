Sacrifice

There is a war aboveground,
and my Brother is starving.
He has not eaten in four days,
He is young and frail, he will die the next day.

There is screaming and shouting,
and we are below ground.
But my Brother is starving,
and I must feed him.

There is screaming and shouting, and we are below ground. But my Brother is starving, and I must feed him.

Nails dug through tough dirt,
I unearth blood, worms, and my parents' dead corpse.
I cannot break the surface.
I cannot remove the dirt (blood).

What can a Sister do?
There is no food underneath the Earth.
What can a young girl do?
There are no living thing waiting aboveground.

I approach my Brother,
clutching his stomach and crying.
I lift the blade that my father discarded.
Lord, I will carry my Brother's sins.

I bleed, and bleed, and bleed.
And offer my sacrifice for him.
My Brother is starving
and I must feed him.

Lord, my soul will bear the crime.
Lord, my flesh will feed him.
My Brother cannot starve,
I have laid a feast.
